Police Crime Fighter

If you ever feel that there is a lot of injustice in this world and nothing is done about it, then this is the game! Solve as many crimes as you can using your provided police resources. All the offences in the game are sourced from actual crimes in Birmingham.

Features

Level: 'basic' policeman can only perform one task at a time.
Level: 'Skilled' Police car can cover a small group of tasks
Level: 'Professional' Police-dog and team can cover a large group of tasks
Level: 'Advanced' Helicopter can cover large area
